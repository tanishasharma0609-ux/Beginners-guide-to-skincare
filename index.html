Dry Skin

Skin feels tight and may flake; benefits from richer hydration and barrier-repair ingredients.

Oily Skin

Prone to shine with visibly enlarged pores; often benefits from lightweight, non-comedogenic formulations.

Combination

Oily in the T-zone and drier elsewhere; balancing routines that address multiple zones are recommended.

Sensitive

Reactive or easily irritated skin that responds best to gentle, fragrance-free care.
