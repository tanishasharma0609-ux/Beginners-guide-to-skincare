Basics

  • Cleanser → Moisturizer → Sunscreen (AM)
  • Cleanser → Treatment/Serum → Moisturizer (PM)
  • Introduce new products slowly & patch-test.

Ingredients to know

  • Hyaluronic Acid: hydration (dry skin)
  • Niacinamide: oil & pores (oily/combination)
  • Salicylic Acid (BHA): acne & blackheads
  • Centella/Panthenol: soothing (sensitive)
