Expert Skincare Guidance

Daily Skincare Tips

  • Cleanse your skin gently twice a day.
  • Always apply sunscreen, even indoors.
  • Hydrate from inside and outside.
  • Exfoliate 1–2 times a week.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Sleeping with makeup on.
  • Over-washing or over-exfoliating.
  • Using too many products at once.
  • Ignoring patch tests for new products.
